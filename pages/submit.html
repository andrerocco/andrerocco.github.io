SUBMIT YOUR OUTFIT PICTURE!

Submit us your outfit picture to be featured on the yeezy catalog


Requirements:

  1. Decent image quality and resolution. Make sure your fit can be seen!
  2. Good environment. Send us an awesome fit pic, not only an awesome outfit!
  3. No major color changing filters, such as a black & white filter, etc.


Send your picture & information to sumbit@yeezy-catalog.com following this format:

Subject: Outfit submission - Your name
The body of the email: Instagram: @yourusername
Yeezy: yeezy model used on the picture
Colorway: colorway of that yeezy model

If the image is posted on instagram, include the link to it at the end of the email.
If the image is not posted on instagram, include the image in the email.
